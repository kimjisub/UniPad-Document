Save the data about **Button Touch and Chain changes** to play the pack automatically. If you pause after turning on automatic playback, you will switch to **Practice Mode**, which also works based on automatic playback data. AutoPlay data can be recorded with the recording function of `UniPad`.

---

## Structure

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="chain" label="chain" default>

**Change** the chain

| chain | `chain` |
| ----- | ------- |
| chain | 3       |
| c     | 3       |

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="on" label="on">

Press the button on the coordinates.

| on  | x   | y   |
| --- | --- | --- |
| on  | 6   | 4   |
| o   | 6   | 4   |

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="off" label="off">

좌표의 눌린 버튼을 땝니다.

| off | x   | y   |
| --- | --- | --- |
| off | 6   | 4   |
| f   | 6   | 4   |

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="touch" label="touch">

Run the on command and off command at **simultaneously**.

| touch | x   | y   |
| ----- | --- | --- |
| touch | 6   | 4   |
| t     | 6   | 4   |

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="delay" label="delay">

Give a **Delay Time** until the next event.

| delay | ms  |
| ----- | --- |
| delay | 120 |
| d     | 120 |

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

```text title="autoPlay"
c 1
o 3 3
d 100
f 3 3
d 100
t 4 4
```

:::info
Events within 20 ms are recognized as **simultaneous touch** when executing practice mode.
:::
